Disease

Unapologetically decadent, the Diseases cupcake is a fearless fusion of dark chocolate and crimson cherry compote, topped with a swirl of black vanilla buttercream and edible glitter. This treat is all about embracing your inner monster. rich and dangerously delicious.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Dark cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Granulated sugar
  • Pitted cherries
  • Black vanilla buttercream
  • Black food coloring (optional)
  • Edible glitter (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Whisk wet ingredients separately, then combine.
  4. Fold in cherries.
  5. Pour into cupcake liners and bake 18–20 minutes.
  6. Cool completely, then frost with black vanilla buttercream and sprinkle with glitter.
